This section records the main limits of the simplified manuscript.

\subsection*{Scope}
The manuscript is a proposed proof framework prepared for mathematical audit.
Only statements with complete proofs under explicit hypotheses count as established.

\subsection*{Open targets}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Target A (open):} prove strip-level correspondence between $\Phi(q)=0$ and nontrivial zeros of $\zeta$.
  \item \textbf{Target B (open):} prove $\Phi(q)=0\Rightarrow \RePart(q)=\tfrac12$ on the strip.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Consequence}
Because Targets~A and~B are open, the manuscript does not claim a proof of RH.
It presents a conditional route and the remaining proof obligations.
