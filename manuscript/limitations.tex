This section records present limits of the manuscript under review.

\subsection*{Scope}
The manuscript is a proposed proof framework.
Only proved statements under explicit hypotheses count as established.

\subsection*{Canonical object and status}
The main text intentionally fixes
\[
\Phi(q):=\Gamma(q)(1-q)\zeta(q)
\]
as the canonical object.
This is a simplifying manuscript decision for audit clarity.
It is not yet proved to be the only or final viable formulation.

\subsection*{Main open theorem targets}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Target A (zero--balance correspondence).}
  Prove that, on the intended strip domain, $\Phi(q)=0$ corresponds exactly to nontrivial zeros of $\zeta$.

  \item \textbf{Target B (critical-line exclusion).}
  Prove that strip solutions of $\Phi(q)=0$ satisfy $\RePart(q)=\tfrac12$.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Consequence for RH claims}
Because Targets A and B remain open, the manuscript does not claim a proof of RH.
It provides materials for mathematical audit of a conditional argument.
